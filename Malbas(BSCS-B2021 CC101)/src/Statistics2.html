STATISTICS

Bodies of Water
         The islands of the Philippines, also known as the Philippine Archipelago, is one of the richest and most beautiful tropical destinations on earth. In total, there are over 7641 islands in the archipelago

Land Area
         The country of the Philippines is an archipelago made up of over islands that total 115,831 square miles.

Regions
         The Philippines is subdivided into seventeen (17) regions - eight (8) in Luzon, three (3) in the Visayas, and six (6) in Mindanao.
